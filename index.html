Our Mission

Like the fossil fuels that keep your car running, the data that keep your product running can lead to harmful side effects. That's why we're building a tool that puts user privacy first while still giving you the insights you need to build a great product. Think the electric car of data analytics.

Data is gold?

Today's tech companies treat data like gold. They gather it, hoard it, and count it, seeing it as a valuable asset with few downsides. Why not track everything and figure it out later?!

  • Tell me more

More like toxic waste!

We think data is a far more toxic element than gold. It's incredibly powerful and valuable, but it's also dangerous when not handled with care. Mishandled data has the potential to harm people for generations to come, and we want to do something about it.

  • Tell me more

Tenets of Clean Insights

That's why we're building Clean Insights, a tool to power and empower privacy-preserving analytics. Using advanced netowrk security, privacy-by-default tools and logic, and cutting edge privacy research methods, Clean Insights creates the new standard for responsible analytics.
