Our Mission

Like the fossil fuels that keep your car running, the data that keep your product running can lead to harmful side effects. That's why we're building a tool that puts user privacy first while still giving you the insights you need to build a great product. Think the electric car of data analytics.

The Scenario

Imagine you run an image-sharing service with end-to-end encryption and a major focus on privacy. To keep up with the other trendy photo apps, you want to build some cool filters, but only for the images your users share most often. You're in a pinch. You can easily determine your needs by running a standard analytics program on individual devices, but that's a lot more data than you want to log.

Aha! You can use Clean Insights to take a differentially private measure of user activity. You won't have to store individual data points and you'll still learn how your users are behaving in aggregate.
